Different Maps of Greenspace Density

Heatmap of Greenspace Density
After retrieving the polygons of the different greenspaces, first examine the density of different greenspace polygons using an interactive heatmap to visualize the different areas where these polygons are located on a map. Darker red areas indicate more greenspace polygons (i.e., more greespaces within that vicinity).
Format the Data
Format the dataset by finding the centroid of each polygon, and then placing those centroids (in red points) onto the census tract polygon for Johnson County.This is how we will examine the number of polygons within each census tract, and how this will be placed onto an interactive choropleth.
