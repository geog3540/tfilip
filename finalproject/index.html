Greenspace Density in Johnson County

Greenspace and Psychosis
Greenspace has been linked to a variety of physiological and psychological outcomes. Here we will create a tool to examine greenspace density and exposure (via distance) to be applied to a dataset of GPS-derived movement data in people at increased risk of developing a psychotic disorder. This tool will be used to examine the potential protective effect that greenspace exposure may have on the risk for the development of psychosis.
